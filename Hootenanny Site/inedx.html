Welcome to the official site for the 2nd Annual Hootenanny Friends & Family Reunion! The goal of this magical gathering is to bring together new friends and old friends alike to catch up and boogie on down like we used to at UNC Asheville.

Let your imagination be your guide...

Come prepared to ho-down! This year's site is on the largest piece of natural white water on the Catawba River. We will be meeting old friends and carrying on all weekend, so bring food and beer! Feel free to bring tents, camping goodies, food, drinks, toys, lights, insturments, boats, floats etc.

Where: Within 40 min of Asheville
Entertainment (tentative):

  • Empire Strikes Brass
  • JP Furnas & Friends Reggae Jam
  • More to come!
  • Don't hesitate to contact us if you have a band you'd like to book for this year's Hootenanny!
